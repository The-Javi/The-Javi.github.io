Overview

This was part of my low speed aerodynamics class final project. My team designed and tested a method to gauge the effectivness of vortex generators on cars. For a little background, vortex generators are usually little tabs that induce turbulence in the boundary layer. Some airplanes use them to keep air attached at high angles of attack, they are used by car enthusiasts, and in wind turbines.
